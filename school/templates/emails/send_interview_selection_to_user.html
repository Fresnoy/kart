Hello {{user.first_name}} {{user.last_name}},

We are pleased to inform you that your application for Le Fresnoy has been pre-selected by our admissions jury.

You are therefore invited to come to Le Fresnoy for the final stage interview by the Grand Jury, to be held on:

{{ interview_date.en|upper }}

Please make sure you are Le Fresnoy at least an hour before your interview time, and to bring identification.

The Grand Jury is distinct from the preliminary jury. It comprises practitioners of several artistic disciplines together with the director of Le Fresnoy. The Grand Jury judges only the video submitted by the applicant. It will ask you about your projects and ideas as well as your motivation.

Given the current health crisis, applicants can choose between a live interview at Le Fresnoy or a video-conference. They must advise the school of their choice before 1 June (acaquant@lefresnoy.net).

Congratulations on passing the first part of the entrance exam. We wish you all good luck for the next stage.
